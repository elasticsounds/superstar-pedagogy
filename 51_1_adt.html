Digital Pedagogy Considerations

Consideration 5: Practice digital information searches, and support learners in managing complex material and mitigating potential overwhelm.

When using generative AI, verify sources and watch for errors or biases.

Tip: Create digital mind-maps together with students to help learners rephrase information in their own words, using age-appropriate resources.

Introduce generative AI gradually, focusing on content that can be cross-checked with reliable sources.
