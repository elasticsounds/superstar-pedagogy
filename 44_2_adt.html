Consideration 1: Supporting learner emotional safety

The digital setting should promote focus, and not detract from learner concentration.

Tips

  • Tip: Engage in dialogue with learners about the appropriateness and responsibility of digital and F2F social interactions.
  • Collaboratively create house rules that recognise the value of constructive disagreements and discussions.
  • Practice providing and evaluating digital feedback, which can later be reviewed for quality.
