Superstar teacher toolbox

Digital pedagogy considerations

Effective use of digital tools requires continuous learning and technological awareness.

Examples of digital competencies in the context of preparing the content

  • Understanding of copyright laws and fair use.
  • Guidance on safe internet browsing and source verification.
  • Response strategies for inappropriate content sharing.
  • Use of content filters and privacy tools.

Digital Work

  • Creation of various digital materials including presentations, videos, and interactive content.
  • Curation of digital resources from online platforms.

Accessibility & Inclusion

  • Reviewing digital content for accessibility.
  • Creating accessible documents with proper formatting.

AI & Analytics

  • Use of adaptive tools for personalized content.
  • Monitoring student engagement and progress with analytics.
  • Evaluation of AI-driven content for effectiveness.
