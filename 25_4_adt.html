Consideration 3: Collegial or other staff member support

How many staff members might there be in your classroom, and how can you share the central aspects of using digital tools in teaching and learning?

Consider shared pedagogical planning (i.e., goals, assessment, contents, assignments, differentiation), testing the tool, rehearsing it with learners, technical maintenance, reporting problems and so on.

Tip: Work together to map out the digital resources available, identifying how they can enhance learning across various subjects or age groups.

Prioritise careful planning to determine potential areas where the teacher may require extra classroom assistance when using a particular tool.
