UNESCO ICT Competency Framework

UNESCOs ICT Competency Framework for Teachers is a tool designed to guide pre- and in-service teacher training on the use of digital technologies in both formal and informal education systems, from K-12 to tertiary level.

It provides a framework within which digital competencies can be identified and used to inform ICT in education policy directives, curriculum design, and pre- and in-service training.

It recognises the digital competencies in the following areas:

  • Understanding ICT in education
  • Curriculum & Assessment
  • Pedagogy
  • Digital Skills
  • Organisation and Administration
  • Professional Teacher Learning
