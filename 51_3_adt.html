Digital Pedagogy Considerations

Consideration 7: Digital tools should facilitate collaborative problem-solving, recognising the value of tackling challenges together.

Problem-solving requires a blend of ideas, perspectives, information, skills, and negotiation, particularly with open-ended issues.

Tip: Many edu-tech games offer group problem-solving opportunities.

Investigate and analyse these options, finding ways to incorporate the benefits of group discussion/brainstorming even where the tool is not inherently collaborative.
