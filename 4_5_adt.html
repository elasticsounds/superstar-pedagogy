The Structure

After the main two chapters, there is an additional third chapter: 3: Additional Information and Resources, which contains useful summaries and material for further reading on central digital skills areas and the theoretical aspects of digital pedagogy.

The chapter includes content in 3.1 Digital Safety, 3.2 Accessibility & Inclusion, 3.3 Digital Competences, 3.4 AI & Analytics, and 3.5 Digital Pedagogy Theory.
